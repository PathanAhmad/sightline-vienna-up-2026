### 4. The two challenge directions

**Challenge 1: Product Passport meets ERP**
Build a functional ERP-integrated prototype using Digital Product Passport / Battery Passport data.

**Challenge 2: AI-Powered Construction Photo Compliance Audit**
Develop a prototype that uses computer vision to assess geo-referenced construction site photos for completeness, compliance, duplicate use, and potential manipulation, and generate a clear deficiency report.
