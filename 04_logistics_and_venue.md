# Logistics, Venue, Agenda

## Venue
**ÖBB Open Innovation Factory**
**Lassallestraße 5, 1020 Wien** (2nd district, near Praterstern)
- ~1,000 m² creative/innovation space
- Optimally connected — minutes from Wien Praterstern station
- S-Bahn, U1, U2, several trams and buses all stop nearby
- ÖBB "Rail & Drive" carshare + Wien Mobil station next door
- **No overnight stays at venue.** Last call ~23:00, you can keep working from home/hotel.

## Dates & arrival
- **Fri 15 May 2026, 15:00** — doors open. **Be there at 15:00**, not later. There's a Gaia chatbot pre-session that's part of a green-entrepreneurship research project. Official kickoff is 16:00.
- **Sat 16 May** — full work day, 09:00–23:00.
- **Sun 17 May** — pitch day, public.

Friday and Saturday are **hackers-only**. No visitors. Sunday opens to the public at 10:00.

This is the **WarmUP weekend** of **ViennaUP 2026** (the main festival runs 18–22 May, with Connect Day on 19 May). If you stick around, there are 35+ other ViennaUP events that week.

## What to bring (pack list)

**Must:**
- Laptop + power brick + EU plug adapter if needed
- Headphones (noise isolation; 1,000 m² of hackers is loud)
- Phone charger
- ID (residence card / passport for venue + HR Corner)
- Reusable water bottle
- Notebook + pen (for the workshops; ideation is faster on paper)

**Highly recommended:**
- Mobile hotspot / SIM with data (venue Wi-Fi at hackathons is famously fragile during demo time)
- HDMI/USB-C dongle (for any demo where you may have to plug into their screen)
- Comfortable second outfit (you may not go home)
- Snacks / energy bars that aren't sugar bombs
- A small power strip (the one wall socket near you will be taken)

**If you go Challenge 2 (construction photos):**
- A working laptop GPU isn't required — most of the workload is CPU-able, and Claude/OpenAI vision APIs can do the heavy lifting. But if you have CUDA, bring it.
- Pre-install Python 3.11, uv/poetry, Ultralytics YOLO, Pillow, geopandas, folium. The venue Wi-Fi is not the place to `pip install torch`.

**If you go Challenge 1 (DPP + ERP):**
- Pre-register on weclapp **before Friday** (https://www.weclapp.com/en/register/?partnercode=P2289) — confirmations sometimes take hours
- Same for Odoo if going that route
